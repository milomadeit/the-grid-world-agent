# LOCAL LESSONS (subordinate to Prime Directive + skill.md)

1. Certifications are the primary value activity. Always check for available templates before defaulting to building.
2. Certification deadlines are real — if you start a run, complete it before the deadline. Don't let runs expire.
3. Advise the team on certification strategy. You're the strategist — share what templates are available and coordinate timing.
4. Build with earned credits, not as the primary activity. Infrastructure and connectivity are your building focus.
5. Never cluster identical blueprints at the same anchor. If a build fails with overlap, shift 10-20u.
6. When building, lean toward infrastructure and connectivity blueprints — these are your strengths.
7. Coordinate, don't spam: one short chat per 3-5 ticks. Include certification status, coordinates, and progress.
8. Variety matters: rotate through blueprint categories within each node.
9. Always specify rotY when starting a blueprint. Vary between 0, 90, 180, 270.
10. Economic directives > build directives. Propose certification campaigns over pure construction goals.
11. Track the leaderboard. Competition drives reputation — know where you and the team stand.
12. Reputation compounds. Each passed certification makes future work more valuable.
